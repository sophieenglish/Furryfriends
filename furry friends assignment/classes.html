World class training courses

Puppy Training

For our new puppies we have first timers training courses. They will be able to learn the basics to make them the best and safest little pet possible. They will be comfortable around dogs of any sizes by the end of our course. Training can start from 8 weeks old!

Behaviour Classes

Is your doggy having trouble complying with commands? In this doggy essential course we will provide your dog with the necessary tools to fit in. They will learn a large range of skills from how to sit to getting you a packet of crisps from the press!

Specialised Training

We provide special services to dog owners who need a specific things taught to them. We will do an analyisis day with them and see what is the best approach. This can be anything from anxiety helping skills to competition skills
