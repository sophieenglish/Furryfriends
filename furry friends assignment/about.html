Beginnings

Furry Friends started off with me, Shelly Attaway, and co-founder, Marco Porgio. We met in dog obedience training school and stayed friends ever since. We are both qualified vets and animal counsellors. We both have a passion for animals and giving them their full potential. Together we started in a much smaller location, only able to house around 10 pets. We have since grown our pet and staff numbers into the dozens.

Hi, I'm Shelly! I am the founder of Furry Friends, we started this business in 2007. I graduated from my vetenarian degree from DCU in 2002 and took courses in Pet Professional Ireland for my dog training qualifications. This has been an amazing journey and I have met so many amazing pets and people along the way. I myself am the owner of three dogs and one cat and I love them very dearly.

I'm Marco Porgio and I am the co-founder of Furry Friends. I am a qualified vet and dog trainer, I met Shelly at a dog training course in 2006 and our friendship has blossomed into the amazing place we run today. Together me and Shelly have created a safe and fun place for dogs and cats of all types. We are still expanding and I am looking forward to all the great animals and people that come through our doors next!
