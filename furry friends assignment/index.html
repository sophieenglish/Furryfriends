Our Services

Behaviour Classes

Is your dog having a hard time interacting with others? Do they sometimes get agitated at small things? This behaviour training course is perfect for those pets that just need the extra push on how to interact with others in their daily life.

Pet Hotel

Furry Friends pet hotel is the best place for them to stay while their loved ones are gone away. Whether it's just for the day or for two weeks, the cats and dogs are in good hands with our trianed staff. They will recieve lots of cuddles and play time!

Puppy Training

Having a new puppy around the house can be a stressful time. They might not know how to interact with a child or pull on the leash during walks. After this training course which is specifically tailored to each pup, they will be the perfect family pet.
